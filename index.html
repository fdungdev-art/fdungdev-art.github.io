Stick War Legacy – Complete Beginner Guide 2025

Your ultimate strategy guide to mastering battles, units, and game modes.

If you’re searching for the best way to start your journey in Stick War Legacy, then this complete beginner guide will give you everything you need. Stick War Legacy has become one of the most popular mobile strategy games because of its simple gameplay, powerful units, and exciting battles across the world of Inamorta.

What Makes Stick War Legacy So Popular?

Stick War Legacy stands out among mobile strategy games because it focuses on both skill and planning. You control every unit manually, making the game more interactive than auto-battle style games. From gathering gold to attacking enemy statues, every action depends on your timing and strategy.

Understanding the World of Inamorta

In Stick War Legacy, all nations worship different weapons. Each kingdom has its own unique fighting style, troops, and strengths. Your mission is to unite these nations by defeating their armies one by one. The more levels you clear, the stronger your enemies become.

Main Units in Stick War Legacy

Each unit in the game has a special role. Here are the most important ones:

1. Swordwrath (Basic Fighter)

A fast melee unit with strong early-game power. Great for defending your statue and attacking early weaknesses.

2. Archidon (Archer)

Long-range units that deal damage from a safe distance. Perfect for weakening enemies before your melee troops engage.

3. Spearton (Elite Defender)

The strongest defensive unit. High armor, shield blocking, and great for holding the front line.

4. Magikill (Wizard)

Powerful support unit that can summon minions and unleash magic attacks. Perfect for late-game battles.

5. Giant (Massive Damage Dealer)

Slow but extremely powerful. Giants are perfect for breaking enemy statues and ending battles quickly.

The Different Game Modes Explained

1. Classic Campaign Mode

This is the main story mode where you conquer different nations. Each mission gets more challenging, and enemies use stronger unit combinations.

2. Tournament Mode

A competitive mode where you fight advanced AI opponents. Winning tournaments gives you special crowns and rewards.

3. Endless Deads Mode

A survival mode where zombies attack in waves. Your goal is to stay alive as long as possible. This mode helps improve your reflexes and army management.

Best Beginner Tips to Win More Battles

1. Upgrade Miners First

Gold is the backbone of your entire army. Upgrading miners first ensures faster troop production and quick strategy execution.

2. Balance Defense and Offense

Having only melee units or only archers will not help you win. Always mix Swordwrath + Archidon or Spearton + Archidon for balanced fights.

3. Use Formation Strategy

Place Speartons in the front, archers in the back, and giants for final pushes. This formation wins most battles easily.

4. Save Gems for Hard Levels

Gems allow you to revive, boost troops, or unlock skins. Use them strategically, especially on difficult campaign missions.

5. Learn When to Attack

Attacking too early can cause your whole army to die. Wait until you have a balanced squad before launching a full offensive.

Common Mistakes Beginners Should Avoid

  • Sending troops without backup
  • Ignoring statue health
  • Not upgrading units regularly
  • Over-relying on only one troop type
  • Wasting giants early in the match
